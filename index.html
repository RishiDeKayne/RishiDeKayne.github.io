Dr. Rishi De-Kayne


I am currently a PostDoc at the University of California Berkeley with Peter Sudmant

My current work is focussed on leverging genomic data, particularly structural variation, to understand the evolution and maintainence of a important phenotypic traits including logevity. I am also working on understanding the evolution of wing-pattern variation in butterflies and the adaptation of Poecilia fish species to toxic, sulfide rich, streams.

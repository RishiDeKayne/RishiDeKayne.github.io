Current Collaborators

Simon Martin

I am currently based in the lab of Dr. Simon Martin working on the origin and maintainence of wing-pattern supergenes in the African monarch butterfly Danaus chrysippus





Philine Feulner

Dr. Philine Feulner was one of my two PhD supervisors. Together we produced new resources for Alpine whitefish Coregonus spp. and carried out a large whole-genome re-sequencing project to investigate the genetic basis of adaptation and speciation across the Alpine whitefish radiation





Ole Seehausen

Prof. Ole Seehausen was the other of my two PhD supervisors. With Ole's help we planned and carried out our large whole-genome re-sequencing project to investigate the genetic basis of adaptation and speciation across the Alpine whitefish radiation





Cameron Hudson

I am currently collaborating on a qtl mapping project with Dr. Cameron Hudson where we aim to understand the genetic basis of key foraging traits in stickleback fishes





Past Collaborators

Vincent Savolainen

During my batchelors and masters degrees I worked in the lab of Prof. Vincent Savolainen. The most substantial proportion of this work involved working on the Howea palm system on Lord Howe island. This project aimed to understand the role of soil bacteria and fungi in promoting or facilitating sympatric speciation of Howea palms.
